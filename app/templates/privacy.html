Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use the David Academy Student Portal, we collect the following information:

  • Full name, date of birth, and class — provided at enrollment
  • Student ID — auto-generated by the system
  • Academic records — grades entered by your teachers
  • Payment records — when school fees are paid through the portal
  • Parent or guardian contact number — provided at enrollment
  • Login activity — date and time of portal access

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for educational and administrative purposes:

  • To identify you uniquely within the school system
  • To display your academic results when the portal is open
  • To process and verify school fee payments
  • To allow teachers to record and update your grades
  • To allow administrators to manage the school system

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes. Ever.

3. Password Security

Your password is never stored in plain text. It is converted into a secure cryptographic hash before being saved to the database. This means even the system administrators cannot see your real password — only you know it.

If you forget your password, you must contact your school administrator to reset it. There is no email recovery because we do not store your email address unless you are a teacher.

4. Payment Data

All payment transactions are processed by Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. David Academy never sees, stores, or has access to your card number or bank account details. We only receive a confirmation that a payment was successful, along with the amount and reference number.

For full details on how Paystack handles your payment data, visit paystack.com/privacy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take the following measures to protect it:

  • All passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptography
  • The portal uses HTTPS encryption in production — all data in transit is encrypted
  • Database access is restricted — only the system can query it, not the public
  • Sessions expire automatically to prevent unauthorized access on shared devices

6. Your Rights

As a student or parent, you have the right to:

  • Request to see the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your account upon graduation or withdrawal

All such requests must be made through your school administrator.

7. Children's Privacy

Many of our users are under 18. We take this seriously. We do not collect any data beyond what is strictly necessary for school administration, and we do not display advertising or share student data with any marketing platforms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated. Significant changes will be communicated through the school administration.

Questions about your privacy? Contact your school administrator or reach us at
